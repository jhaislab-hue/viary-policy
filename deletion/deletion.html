Account Deletion Policy

Please find the instructions in your language below.

English

You can request the deletion of your Viary account and associated data in two ways:

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

This is the fastest way to delete your account.

  1. Open the Viary app and go to 'Account' > 'Account management' > 'Delete Account'.
  2. Follow the on-screen verification steps.

Process: Your account will be immediately disabled (you will not be able to log in), and a 30-day grace period will begin. After 30 days, your data will be permanently deleted.

Method 2: Email Request (Web)

Use this method if you no longer have access to the app.

  1. Send an email to: jhaislab@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. In the email, please include the email address or User ID (UID) of the account.

Process: We will process your request within 7 business days. Once processed, your account will be immediately disabled, and the 30-day grace period will begin. After 30 days, your data will be permanently deleted.
