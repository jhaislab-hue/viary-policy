Viary Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 25, 2025
Last Updated: October 25, 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Viary ("we", "us", the "App") collects, uses, stores, shares, and deletes personal information, and informs you of your rights. We follow global privacy principles (data minimization, purpose limitation, storage limitation, integrity/confidentiality, transparency).

2. Information We Collect and How

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide the service.

Account Information (optional)

  • Items: Email, profile name/photo, user ID (UID), social login provider info (e.g., Google)
  • Method: When you sign up/sign in via Firebase Authentication

Subscription & Payments

  • Items: Active entitlements, purchase status, restore history (we do NOT collect card details)
  • Method: Via RevenueCat SDK and the Apple App Store/Google Play billing systems

Support/Contact

  • Items: Category, subject/body, app version, OS/device info, UID
  • Method: When you contact us via email or in-app inquiry

3. How We Use Information

To provide recording/memo/visualization features, manage accounts (authentication/login/enforcement), handle subscriptions and purchases, provide support, and improve the service (statistics, debugging, security, and new features).

4. Retention

We process/retain data only for lawful periods or as consented.

  • Account: Upon your deletion request, your account is immediately disabled. Following a 30-day grace period, your account and all associated data are permanently deleted. This grace period is set to prevent fraudulent use and to support account recovery.
  • Subscription/Payment records: Retained per applicable e-commerce/tax laws (typically 5 years)
  • Consumer complaints/dispute records: Retained per applicable laws (typically 3 years)
  • Principle: Voice files are stored on your device under your control. They are not stored on our servers and cannot be recovered after you uninstall the App.
  • Exception (AI features): If you choose AI analysis/summary, voice data is sent ephemerally to Google Gemini API and deleted immediately after processing.

5. Deletion Procedures & Methods

We delete data without undue delay when the retention period ends or the purpose is fulfilled. Electronic files are irreversibly deleted; paper records are shredded/incinerated.

As an exception, for account deletion requests (per Article 4, Item 1), the account is immediately disabled, and the permanent destruction process will begin after the 30-day grace period has elapsed.

6. Processors (Service Providers)

  • Google LLC (Firebase Authentication): Sign-up/sign-in authentication
  • RevenueCat, Inc.: Subscription and entitlement management
  • Apple Inc., Google LLC: App marketplace billing
  • Google LLC (Gemini Developer API): AI processing when you opt in

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to/processed in countries such as the United States where our service providers are located. We use appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) to protect your data.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or object to processing. You may also withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
